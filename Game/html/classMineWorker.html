Detailed Description

The MineWorker class represents a mine worker in the game.

Worker has following production-efficiency:

  • Money - 0.25
  • Food - 0.25
  • Wood - 0.25
  • Stone - 1.50
  • Ore - 1.50
    MineWorkers consume Food and money.
  • 2 Food - Or MineWorker refuses to work.
  • 2 Money - Or MineWorker works at 50% efficiency.
