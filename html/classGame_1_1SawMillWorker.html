Detailed Description

The SawMillWorker class represents a saw mill worker in the game.

Worker has following production-efficiency:

  • Money - 0.25
  • Food - 0.25
  • Wood - 2.00
  • Stone - 0.25
  • Ore - 0.25
    SawMillWorker consume Food and money.
  • 2 Food - Or SawMillWorker refuses to work.
  • 2 Money - Or SawMillWorker works at 50% efficiency.
